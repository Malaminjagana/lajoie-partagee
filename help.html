Help & Support

If you are experiencing issues with our website, donations, or volunteer forms, this page provides guidance and contact information.

1. Website Issues

If pages are not loading correctly or you encounter errors, please try:

  • Refreshing the page.
  • Clearing your browser cache.
  • Trying a different browser or device.

2. Donation Problems

If a donation does not go through or you see an error message from our payment provider, please:

  • Check that your payment details are correct.
  • Ensure your card or PayPal account is active and authorized.
  • Contact us with the date and approximate time of the attempt.

3. Volunteer Form

If you submitted a volunteer form and did not receive a confirmation, please:

  • Check your spam or junk email folder.
  • Verify that you entered the correct email address.
  • Contact us directly if the issue persists.
