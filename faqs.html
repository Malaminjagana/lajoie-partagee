Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is La Joie Partagée?

La Joie Partagée is a charitable initiative focused on supporting vulnerable children, especially those living on the streets, by providing food, emotional support, and opportunities for a better future.

2. How can I make a financial contribution?

You can make a financial contribution through our secure payment or donation section on the website. All payments are processed by trusted third‑party providers, and we do not store full payment card details on our servers.

3. How do I become a volunteer?

You can apply to become a volunteer by filling out the volunteer form on our website. Once submitted, our team will review your message and contact you with further information.

4. How is my personal data used?

We only use your personal data to communicate with you, process your support, and improve our services. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.

5. Can I get a receipt for my contribution?

Yes. If you require a receipt or confirmation for your contribution, please contact us with the date, amount, and method of payment, and we will provide appropriate documentation where possible.
